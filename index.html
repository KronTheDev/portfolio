The Liminal

Countless levels stretch beyond the edge of maps—corridors, rooms, blinked spaces that should not exist. On release there are 15 mapped levels; each is meant to disorient, to hold you just long enough to make you doubt whether you ever left.

The Unknown

They watch. The Howler, the Hounds and the Duller—each with their own predatory patience—study your patterns and wait for the single, fatal mistake. They are not always loud; sometimes the quiet is the loudest warning.

The Unforseen

You were not supposed to find this. Abandoned by a government body that once swore to map the Halls, our protagonist follows breadcrumbs of a past operation and discovers something older, something that never belonged in any report. The Player uncovers it piece by piece.

Confirmed Stuff

Mapped Levels

15 levels will be included at initial release — each with unique hazards, architecture, and audio ambiance.

Known Entities

Confirmed inhabitants: the Howler, the Hounds, and the Duller. Each has distinct behavior and detection mechanics.

Story Outline

Players follow a discovery thread after being abandoned by the MEG-like organization. Gradual reveal: a deeper presence at the heart of the Halls.

Audio & Video

The header uses a looping video with its own audio track. Audio fades in/out based on viewport visibility and requires an interaction if browser policy blocks autoplay.

Release Window

Planned approximate window: Q4 2025 (subject to change).
